About Me

Strategic Senior UX Consultant & Creative Leader with over 20 years of experience driving digital transformation, User Experience governance, and persuasion engineering. I have a proven track record of leading cross-functional teams to deliver mobile-first, responsive digital solutions for global Fortune 500 clients including American Express, The Walt Disney Company, Abbott Laboratories, Walgreens, Novartis, Eli Lilly, ADP, Nielsen IQ, Warner Bros. Discovery, and KeyBank. I specialize in bridging the gap between complex business requirements and user-centric design to drive measurable impact.

Experience

Currently serving as Principal Engineer, Experience Design at Harman Connected Services (a Wipro subsidiary), I lead the UX strategy and design lifecycle for clients like Ellucian, a global leader in higher education technology. I direct the transition to mobile-first responsive platforms, unifying fragmented student workflows into seamless digital experiences. I also advised senior leadership at Carestream Dental (USA) during onsite assessments, identifying high-impact UX improvements and building high-performance design teams.

Previously, as Senior Associate Visual UI/UX Designer & Project Lead at Cognizant Technology Solutions, I led a team of 6 associates for the end-to-end responsive redesign of the American Express (UK) ecosystem — launching the brand's first responsive site in the UK and significantly enhancing cross-platform user engagement. I also managed project estimation and resource allocation across multiple global locations for clients including Disney and Novartis.

Earlier in my career, as Senior UI Specialist at Savitr Software Services, I led the creation of brand identities and marketing strategies, delivering high-performing e-commerce and CMS platforms through direct client interaction and strategic design.
