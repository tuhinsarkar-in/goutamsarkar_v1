Leadership & Management – My Approach, My Practice

Who I Am as a Leader

I am a strategic design leader with over 20 years of experience driving digital transformation, UX governance, and persuasion engineering for global Fortune 500 organizations such as American Express, Disney, Novartis, Abbott Laboratories, Walgreens, Eli Lilly, ADP, Warner Bros, Nielsen IQ, Discovery, KeyBank, Mazda, PSA Group, Nokia, and many others.

Leadership, for me, is not about title — it is about ownership. I take responsibility for outcomes, people, process, and long-term impact. I lead by combining structured thinking with creative direction, ensuring that business strategy and user-centered design work together instead of competing.

My Leadership Philosophy

I believe strong leadership stands on three pillars:

1. Strategic Clarity

I bridge complex business requirements with user-centric solutions. I help stakeholders move from abstract ideas to measurable digital impact. Whether I'm advising C-suite leaders on usability ROI or building SaaS ecosystem roadmaps, I focus on aligning vision with execution.

2. Operational Discipline

Creativity without structure collapses. I build governance systems:

  • Atomic Design systems
  • UI style guides
  • SDLC integration frameworks
  • Clear documentation standards

I ensure project planning, estimation, and resource allocation are precise. I lead cross-location teams and consistently meet 100% of project SLAs.

3. People-Centered Leadership

I mentor designers, scale design teams, and define global hiring standards. I believe high-performance culture comes from clarity, accountability, and continuous improvement. I build teams that meet a high bar for both creative excellence and business excellence.
